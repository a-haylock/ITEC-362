Who We Are

Our Mission Statement: The mission of Teens with a Purpose is to create a space that empowers the youth to speak their truths through creativity and reflection, while using action to growth and change lives and influence communities.

History

Teens with a Purpose was founded in 1996 by Deirdre Love. In 2007, the organization officially became a non-profit youth organization in Norfolk. The vision of Teens with a Purpose started to be an outreach program that addressed contemporary topics including STDs, HIV/AIDs awareness, and more. TWP has celebrated over 27 years of peer leadership and has been awarded regionally and nationally.
