Upcoming Events

The Youth Poet Laureate Program for Hampton Roads and Virginia give teens aged 13-19 the opportunity to display their talents in this region. Pre-registration opens soon for teens who are interested. If you’re interested or someone else, this event will positively influence others using poetry. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us at info@twpthemovement.org.

Programs and Community Outreach

Fuse Fest

The FUSE Fest celebrates positivity and hope within our community by using art, music, culture, and other activities. This event is held yearly and always attracts the community.

Teen Camps

These camps are held every spring and summer and are led by teens and young adults. It covers topics like self-esteem, self-discipline, STD's, and HIV awareness. It typically includes workshops involving creative expression and field trips to activities like museums, glass blowing, kayaking, and more

Garden of Amity

This garden aims to combine peace, harmony, and service. It’s set in a community in Downtown Norfolk. The non-profit sees to the garden, preparing it for each season. It creates a space for nutrition, garden art, and planting seeds.
